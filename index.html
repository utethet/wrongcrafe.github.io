Welcome to the Bizarre World

WrongCraft是一个基于Minecraft 1.19.2的模组包，以"恐怖渗透+奇幻错位"为核心概念设计。在这里，熟悉的方块世界被扭曲重构，每一次探索都隐藏着颠覆与荒诞的惊喜。生存的本质变为在怪诞与恐惧的夹缝中寻找真相。

Welcome to the bizarre world of WrongCraft — a Minecraft modpack based on version 1.19.2, designed with the core concept of "Horror Infiltration + Fantasy Dislocation". Here, the familiar blocky world is twisted and reconstructed, with every exploration hiding subversive and bizarre surprises. The essence of survival becomes finding the truth in the gap between weirdness and fear.

核心玩法：错位世界的恐怖生存

Core Gameplay: Horror Survival in a Dislocated World

恐怖生物狩猎

Horror Creature Hunting

模组包整合了三大玩法维度：恐怖生物狩猎、奇幻魔法生存和荒诞维度探索，在"熟悉又陌生"的Minecraft中为你带来极致的生存压力：

挖矿时可能被Player Mobs中类玩家敌对生物伏击
建造基地时可能被The Knocker中类人潜行者尾随，陷入无法逃脱的追逐
探索其他维度如The Aether和The Deep Void时，可能被SCP-096和The Man-From-The-Fog等超自然存在逼入绝境

The modpack integrates three gameplay dimensions: horror creature hunting, fantasy magic survival, and bizarre dimension exploration, offering you an extreme survival pressure in a "familiar yet strange" Minecraft:

You might be ambushed by player-like hostile mobs from Player Mobs while mining ores
You could be stalked by the human-like stalker from The Knocker when building your base, falling into an inescapable chase
Even when exploring other dimensions like The Aether and The Deep Void, you could be driven into a desperate situation by supernatural beings such as SCP-096 and The Man-From-The-Fog

奇幻系统：在恐惧中寻找生存

Fantasy System: Finding Survival in Fear

利用奇幻魔法和实用模组，在恐怖世界中找到生存的希望。

植物魔法(Botania)：提供自然魔法科技树，使用植物能量构建防御屏障，制作对抗恐怖生物的装备
JEI物品管理器：在混乱中理清合成逻辑
连锁挖矿(Vein Miner)：加速资源获取
精致背包 & 旅行者背包：在恐怖追逐中携带"家当"，将"生存基建"化为对抗荒诞的底气

Utilize fantasy magic and practical mods to find hope for survival in a terrifying world.

Botania: Provides a natural magic tech tree. Use plant energy to build defensive barriers and craft items to counter horror creatures.
Just Enough Items (JEI): Helps you sort out crafting logic in chaos.
Vein Miner: Accelerates resource acquisition.
Sophisticated Backpacks & Traveler's Backpack: Let you carry your belongings while fleeing from horror chases.
